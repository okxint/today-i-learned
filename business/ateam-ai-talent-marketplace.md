# A.Team: AI-Powered Talent Marketplace

**Date:** 2026-03-10

A.Team is a startup building an AI-powered marketplace that assembles elite freelance tech teams on demand. Instead of traditional staffing, they use AI to match pre-vetted "Super Builders" to project needs — giving companies senior-level engineering and product talent without long-term hires.

**Why it matters:** The model sits at the intersection of AI and the future of work. As companies move toward flexible, project-based hiring, platforms that can intelligently match talent to needs will capture significant value.
