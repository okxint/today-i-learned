# Live Nation Antitrust Trial Begins

**Date:** 2026-03-10

The U.S. government's antitrust trial against Live Nation and Ticketmaster opened in New York this month. Prosecutors argue the company uses anticompetitive practices that harm artists, venues, and fans. If found guilty, they could be forced to break up.

**Why it matters:** This could fundamentally reshape the live music industry. Ticketmaster controls ~80% of major venue ticketing. A breakup would open the market to competitors and potentially lower ticket prices.
