# Apple's Siri Relaunch Powered by Google Gemini

**Date:** 2026-03-10

Apple is relaunching Siri with iOS 26.4 this month, powered by Google's Gemini model (1.2T parameters). The twist — it runs on Apple's Private Cloud Compute infrastructure, keeping user data private despite using a competitor's model.

**Why it matters:** Apple chose to partner with a rival rather than ship an inferior in-house model. Privacy as a wrapper around third-party AI could become a pattern other companies follow.
