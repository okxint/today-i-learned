# AI Video Generation Tools

**Date:** 2026-03-10

## Top free/freemium AI video tools

| Tool | Best for | Free tier |
|------|----------|-----------|
| CapCut | Editing with AI features | Fully free |
| Pika | Short AI-generated clips | Limited daily generations |
| Kling AI | Text-to-video | Daily free credits |
| Luma Dream Machine | Quick video generation | ~30 free/month |
| Runway Gen-3 | High-quality generation | 125 credits on signup |

## Key takeaway

CapCut is the most generous free option for editing. For AI generation from text, Pika and Kling AI offer daily free credits.
