# Russell Wins F1 2026 Season Opener

**Date:** 2026-03-10

George Russell won the Australian Grand Prix on March 8 — the first race under F1's new 2026 regulations. Mercedes locked out the podium with Antonelli in P2. The race had 120 overtakes. Verstappen compared the new cars to "Formula E on steroids."

**Why it matters:** New regs emphasize electrical power output, completely reshuffling the competitive order. Mercedes is back. The driver experience is polarizing — this could define whether the new era is a hit or a miss with fans.
