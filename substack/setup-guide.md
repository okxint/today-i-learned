## 4. About Page

---

I'm Ashwin. I build products, write code, and obsess over what's moving in tech, business, and sport.

The Daily Signal is where I break down the one thing worth paying attention to — whether that's a $690B AI infrastructure bubble, a Champions League VAR disaster, or a startup that just raised at a valuation that makes no sense.

Every piece synthesizes 5-12 sources into something you can actually form an opinion from. No hot takes without receipts. No summaries of summaries.

What you'll get:
- Deep dives on AI, startups, SaaS, and crypto (the stuff that affects your career and portfolio)
- Football analysis when something genuinely wild happens (Champions League, IPL, F1)
- A "My Take" section in every piece because I'd rather be wrong and honest than safe and boring

I publish every other day. Twice a week, I pick the best pieces and send them as a Substack email.

Previously: PwC US Advisory, Wipro, Polygon. MBA from IIM Kozhikode. Building in public at github.com/okxint.
